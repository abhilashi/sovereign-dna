## 2024-04-10 - ARIA Labels for Icon-Only Buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (like the `→` submit button in Ask/Workbench, or the `+`/`-` zoom controls in GenomeMap) are easily overlooked for accessibility, leaving screen readers with no context on their functionality.
**Action:** Always verify that buttons lacking descriptive text have an explicit `aria-label` attribute.
