## 2026-04-07 - Focus Management for Action Prompts\n**Learning:** When users click a quick prompt or action button that populates an input field without auto-submitting, they expect to immediately review and hit Enter, but often find their keyboard focus lost. This leads to frustrating mouse-repositioning to re-focus the input.\n**Action:** Whenever a UI element programmatically populates a primary text input, always append `setTimeout(() => inputRef.current?.focus(), 0)` to gracefully shift keyboard focus to that input.
